What is Robotics?

Robotics is a field of engineering and science that deals with the design, construction, and use of robots to perform tasks automatically or semi-automatically.

Core Components of a Robot

  • Sensors – to detect the environment
  • Controller – the brain (microcontroller)
  • Actuators – motors and movement
  • Power Supply – batteries or adapters
  • Structure – chassis and body

Uses of Robotics

  • Education and learning
  • Industrial automation
  • Healthcare and surgery
  • Home automation

Robotics Kit (India)

A beginner robotics kit in India usually costs between ₹1,500 – ₹5,000, depending on components and quality.

Typical Robotics Kit Contents
Component Description
Microcontroller Arduino / compatible board
Motors DC or servo motors
Sensors IR, ultrasonic, line sensor
Chassis Robot body structure
Power Batteries and holders
